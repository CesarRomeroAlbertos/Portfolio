About me

I'm the kind of programmer that likes to work smart. My focus is always on planning projects and designing code such that, instead of creating a solution to a problem we end up with a tool to solve all sort of problems in the current project as well as future ones. Besides that I also enjoy developing tools to help streamline the development process and make difficult and slow tasks fast and automatic. Basically, I'm a power up for your whole team, making it so we can all focus on making the games of our dreams instead of fighting the engine.
